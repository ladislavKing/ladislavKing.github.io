Creative Workshops

Social inclusion is the process through which we value all individuals and recognise their diverse contributions to communities. All children have the right and entitlement to be socially included in their school and/or educational setting. One of the aims of the Creative Workshops is to ensure that partner agencies work together to provide equality of opportunity for all children and young people to participate in and benefit from learning.
